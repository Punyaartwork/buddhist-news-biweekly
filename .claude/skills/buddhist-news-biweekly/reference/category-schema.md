# Category Schema — buddhist-news-biweekly

Every candidate must be tagged with exactly ONE of these 7 categories. The category drives both the Brief layout and the skill routing in `skill-routing-map.md`.

---

## The 7 categories

### 1. Heritage & Archaeology
Archaeological finds, restoration of temples/stupas, UNESCO Buddhist site news, conservation, museum exhibitions.

### 2. Notable Figures
Prominent monks, abbots, lamas, scholars; passings (มรณภาพ); appointments; ordinations of public figures; teacher anniversaries.

### 3. Scholarship
New translations of the Tipitaka / Mahayana scriptures, academic papers, manuscript discoveries, dharma-philosophy debates.

### 4. Cultural & Festivals
Vesak, Asalha, Kathina, Magha Puja, regional Buddhist festivals worldwide.

### 5. Cross-cultural Buddhism
Buddhism in the West, mindfulness science, Buddhism-and-technology, interfaith dialogue, chaplaincy programs.

### 6. Politics & Society
Sangha-state relations, monk activism, refugee monks, temple zoning disputes, Buddhist responses to humanitarian crises. Neutral framing only.

### 7. Controversies *(default OFF)*
Scandals, sectarian disputes, abuse cases, financial irregularities, doctrinal accusations. Skipped automatically when `include_controversies: false`. Even when enabled, requires TWO independent sources.

## Counting rules

- A candidate is counted exactly once. If it fits two categories, pick the one most useful for skill routing (see `skill-routing-map.md`).
- Duplicates across sources (same story, different outlet) are merged into one pick, with the secondary outlet listed under `Source` as "(also covered by X)".
